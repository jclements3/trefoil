## 8. Known limitations

- **Non-diatonic chord labels** -- when an original ABC `"^X"` annotation uses a chord root outside the lever-harp key, `parse_chord_name()` returns `None` and that event is skipped (the rewriter re-emits the original label unchanged). Currently 9713 of 9946 (97.7%) chord events are parseable. Roughly 54 hymns ship with at least one non-diatonic label; another agent is expanding the parser to emit off-key roots as accidentals.
- **Meter exceptions** -- 26 hymns are skipped today: 17 `M:none`, 7 `M:3/2`, and 2 `M:8/4`. Another agent is extending the meter handler to cover the compound / irregular cases.
- **Melody + chord-fractions only** -- the current output is a lead sheet with stacked RH/LH Roman labels above the melody. The pipeline does not (yet) emit realized harp voicings onto separate staves. That is handled by the Tch-SSAATTBBP composed MEI pipeline, which is independent.
