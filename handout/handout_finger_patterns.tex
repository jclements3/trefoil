\textbf{Drill routine:}
\begin{itemize}\setlength{\itemsep}{1mm}
\item \textbf{Unison:} both hands play the same pattern in parallel.
\item \textbf{Mirror:} one hand plays forward, the other reverse (1234 \(\leftrightarrow\) 4321).
\item \textbf{Contrary:} pick one pattern from a group for each hand; alternate anchor roles.
\item \textbf{Cross-size:} one hand on a 4-fng pattern, the other on a 3-fng or 2-fng pattern.
\item \textbf{Independent rhythm:} slow hand anchors while fast hand cycles; then swap.
\end{itemize}

\vspace{2mm}
\fontsize{11}{14}\selectfont
\textit{Notation: digits 1--4 are thumb--index--middle--ring. A split pair \texttt{12\(\vert\)34} means fingers 1 \& 2 strike together, then 3 \& 4 together; \texttt{1\(\vert\)23} means 1 alone, then 2 \& 3. Sequences play each finger in order; pairs play two fingers simultaneously.}
