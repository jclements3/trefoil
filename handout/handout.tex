{\Large\bfseries Trefoil Drill}

\vspace{0.1in}
{\large
The trefoil voicing system uses 48 asymmetric LH/RH chord pairs covering all 12 finger patterns across 6 sections.

\vspace{0.08in}
\textbf{Traversal:} CCW4\,\textrightarrow\,CW3\,\textrightarrow\,CCW2\,\textrightarrow\,CW2\,\textrightarrow\,CCW3\,\textrightarrow\,CW4

\vspace{0.08in}
\textbf{Leaves:}\\
\textcolor{leafcyan}{\rule{1em}{0.8em}} \textbf{2nds} --- stepwise motion\\
\textcolor{leafred}{\rule{1em}{0.8em}} \textbf{3rds} --- skip motion\\
\textcolor{leafyellow}{\rule{1em}{0.8em}} \textbf{4ths} --- circle of fifths

\vspace{0.08in}
\textbf{Time:} 8/4 = 8 chords per measure, one section per bar. Set levers for your key, then play --- no accidentals.

\vspace{0.08in}
\textbf{Keys:} E\fl, B\fl, F, C, G, D, A
}
\end{minipage}
