positions: raise Eb lever (giving E natural) and raise Ab lever (giving
A natural). The two raised levers produce D major (D E F# G A B C#).

Remaining diatonic notes F# and C# must also be lever-set; the full
D major configuration is: C#, D, E, F#, G, A, B — five levers raised
from the all-flats Eb base (Ab→A, Bb→B, Eb→E, and the F# and C# strings
which require those levers engaged in addition to the two named above).
Concretely: all seven strings of D major are available without mid-piece
lever changes.
