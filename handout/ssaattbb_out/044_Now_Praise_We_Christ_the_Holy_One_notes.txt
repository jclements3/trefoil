─────────────

an antiphonal exchange: TTBB leads on beat 1 (Am–F color), SSAA rejoins on

antiphonal SSAA/TTBB exchange (bar 6), call-and-response outer voices
(bar 8), canonic parallel descent in voice pairs (bar 10), and sustained
full-chord final (bar 11).
