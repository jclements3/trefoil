# Practice Form Check

100 points is a perfect score on the Form Check.

1. Height - Sit tall at the strings. Are you sitting at the right height?
2. Posture - Straight spine, open chest. Is your back straight?
3. Shoulders - Let them drop. Are they relaxed, not "hunched" up?
4. Arms - Float the arms. Are they level and relaxed?
5. Elbows - Wings out. Are they lifted away from your body?
6. Wrists - Flat bridge. Are they level, left wrist not on board?
7. Hands - Cup of water. Are they rounded, cupped?
8. Thumbs - Thumbs to the sky. Are your thumbs pointing upward?
9. Fingers - Curl and hang. Are they curved downward and relaxed?
10. Nails - File before you play. Are they filed?
11. Breathing - Breathe through the phrase. Are you breathing naturally, not holding your breath?
12. Harp angle - Lean it back, shoulder the weight. Is the harp resting on your right shoulder, tilted back enough?
13. Feet - Roots in the ground. Are both feet flat on the floor?
14. Closing - Close into the palm. Are you closing fingers into the palm after plucking, not flicking outward?
15. Tension - Soften the jaw. Are your jaw, neck, and face relaxed?
